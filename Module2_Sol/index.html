The dog that bit a cat

Preamble There once was a girl named divy. She was so sweet that it made people giddy. She was kind to all living things, humans and animals apart, but little did she know that this was going to break her heart.

The story The worst for her was her friend turned wolf, and the blood that splattered as she ran. The worst for her: the hospital, her upper lip tugged back to show the gash—the flesh halved deeply, cleanly—while she was held for the needle that rubs pain out. she submits to the quick stitch, the thread black against pink skin, calm now she sees the doctor can be trusted, his voice soothing, his face clean shaven, the clues that signal kindness to a child. she’s worried, though, about her pet as she says he didnt mean it mom.
